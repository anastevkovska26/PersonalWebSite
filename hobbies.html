One of my hobbies is reading.
I have loved reading since I was a little girl.
It makes me calm, happy and with every book I can learn something new.

I started playing together with my brother because he wanted to, but I continued because I wanted to.
Now I love playing the guitar, and I play at every family gathering.

I started playing with my father.
It makes me happy every time I play, because I remember those days.

I have been singing since I was a little girl.

then I feel the most comfortable.
